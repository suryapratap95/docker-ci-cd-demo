FIXED DEPOSITS - ABC BANK

Regular Fixed Deposit:
- Minimum deposit amount: Rs. 5,000
- Tenure options: 7 days to 10 years
- Interest rates: 4.5% to 7.25% per annum depending on tenure (as of last published rate card)
- Premature withdrawal: Allowed with a penalty of 1% on the applicable rate
- Interest payout options: Monthly, quarterly, or on maturity (cumulative)

Senior Citizen Fixed Deposit:
- Same tenure options as Regular Fixed Deposit
- Additional interest rate of 0.5% over the regular rate card
- Minimum deposit amount: Rs. 5,000

Tax-Saving Fixed Deposit:
- Minimum deposit amount: Rs. 10,000
- Fixed tenure: 5 years, no premature withdrawal allowed
- Interest rate: 6.5% per annum
- Tax benefit: Eligible for deduction up to Rs. 1,50,000 under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act
- Interest earned is taxable as per the depositor's income tax slab

Recurring Deposit:
- Minimum monthly installment: Rs. 500
- Tenure options: 6 months to 10 years
- Interest rates: Same as Regular Fixed Deposit for the corresponding tenure
- Missed installment penalty: Rs. 1.50 per Rs. 100 per month of default

Loan Against Fixed Deposit:
Customers can avail a loan or overdraft against their fixed deposit up to 90% of the deposit value. The interest rate charged is typically 1-2% above the fixed deposit interest rate. This is a convenient option since it does not require breaking the deposit prematurely.

TDS on Fixed Deposits:
Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) is applicable if the total interest earned across all fixed deposits with the bank exceeds Rs. 40,000 in a financial year (Rs. 50,000 for senior citizens). Customers can submit Form 15G or 15H to avoid TDS if their total income is below the taxable limit.

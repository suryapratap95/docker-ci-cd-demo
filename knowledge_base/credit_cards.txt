CREDIT CARDS - ABC BANK

ABC Classic Credit Card:
- Annual fee: Rs. 500, waived if annual spend exceeds Rs. 50,000
- Reward points: 1 point per Rs. 100 spent
- Cash withdrawal limit: Up to 20% of credit limit
- Interest rate on revolving credit: 3.5% per month (42% per annum)
- Credit limit: Rs. 25,000 to Rs. 2,00,000 based on income and credit score

ABC Platinum Credit Card:
- Annual fee: Rs. 2,500, waived if annual spend exceeds Rs. 2,00,000
- Reward points: 2 points per Rs. 100 spent, 4 points on dining and travel
- Complimentary airport lounge access: 4 visits per quarter
- Interest rate on revolving credit: 3.25% per month (39% per annum)
- Credit limit: Rs. 1,00,000 to Rs. 10,00,000 based on income and credit score

ABC Signature Credit Card:
- Annual fee: Rs. 10,000, waived if annual spend exceeds Rs. 5,00,000
- Reward points: 3 points per Rs. 100 spent, 6 points on dining and travel
- Unlimited airport lounge access worldwide
- Complimentary travel insurance up to Rs. 1,00,00,000
- Interest rate on revolving credit: 3.0% per month (36% per annum)

Billing and Payment:
Credit card statements are generated monthly. The payment due date is 20 days after the statement date. A minimum amount due of 5% of the outstanding balance must be paid to avoid late payment charges. Late payment charges range from Rs. 100 to Rs. 1,300 depending on the outstanding amount.

Reward Points Redemption:
Reward points can be redeemed for statement credit, merchandise, or travel bookings through the ABC Bank rewards portal. Points expire 3 years from the date of accrual. 100 reward points are typically equivalent to Rs. 25 in statement credit.

Lost or Stolen Cards:
Customers should immediately call the 24x7 helpline or block the card through the mobile app if a card is lost or stolen. There is zero liability for fraudulent transactions reported within 3 days of the unauthorized transaction, provided the customer has not shared their card PIN or OTP with anyone.

How to Apply:
Credit cards can be applied for online through the ABC Bank website or mobile app. Existing customers with a good banking relationship may receive pre-approved offers. New applicants need to submit income proof, ID proof, and address proof. Approval usually takes 3-7 business days.

SAVINGS ACCOUNT - ABC BANK

Overview:
ABC Bank offers three types of savings accounts: Basic Savings, Premium Savings, and Senior Citizen Savings.

Basic Savings Account:
- Minimum balance requirement: Rs. 1,000
- Interest rate: 3.0% per annum, calculated daily and credited quarterly
- Free debit card included
- Free passbook and 2 free chequebooks per year
- No account opening fee

Premium Savings Account:
- Minimum balance requirement: Rs. 25,000
- Interest rate: 3.5% per annum, calculated daily and credited quarterly
- Free premium debit card with higher withdrawal limits
- Complimentary locker discount of 20%
- Dedicated relationship manager

Senior Citizen Savings Account:
- Minimum balance requirement: Rs. 500
- Interest rate: 4.0% per annum (0.5% higher than Basic Savings)
- No minimum balance penalty
- Free doorstep banking services

How to Open an Account:
Customers can open a savings account by visiting any ABC Bank branch with a valid government ID proof, address proof, and two passport-size photographs. Accounts can also be opened online through the ABC Bank mobile app with video KYC verification, usually completed within 15 minutes.

Minimum Balance Penalty:
If the average monthly balance falls below the required minimum, a penalty of Rs. 100 to Rs. 500 is charged depending on the shortfall amount. Senior Citizen accounts are exempt from this penalty.

Closing an Account:
Accounts can be closed by submitting a written request at the branch. Accounts closed within 14 days of opening incur no charges. Accounts closed after 14 days but within 1 year incur a closure fee of Rs. 500.
